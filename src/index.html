1. King Penguin
    • Second-largest species.
    • Found in sub-Antarctic regions.
    • Their breeding season is different from the Emperor Penguin.

    Other Types of Penguins:

    • Chinstrap Penguin - Known for the distinctive black band under their chin.
    • Macaroni Penguin - Recognizable by their yellow "moustache."
    • Little Blue Penguin - The smallest species, standing at only 33 cm (13 inches).
